# Goochland GOP Newsletter Style Guidelines

## Voice and Tone

### Voice
- **Professional**: Maintain credibility through well-researched, fact-based content
- **Conservative**: Reflect Republican values and principles authentically
- **Community-Focused**: Prioritize local Goochland County connections
- **Accessible**: Write for a general audience, avoiding jargon

### Tone
- **Informative**: Educate readers with clear, accurate information
- **Respectful**: Treat all perspectives with dignity
- **Constructive**: Focus on solutions and positive action
- **Engaging**: Write in an active, conversational style

## Writing Standards

### Language
- Use AP Style for formatting and grammar
- Write at an 8th-10th grade reading level
- Avoid political jargon and acronyms without explanation
- Use active voice whenever possible
- Be specific and concrete rather than abstract

### Structure
1. **Headline**: 60-80 characters, compelling and informative
2. **Lead**: Hook readers in the first paragraph with the most important information
3. **Body**: Develop the story with facts, examples, and analysis
4. **Local Connection**: Always explain relevance to Goochland residents
5. **Call-to-Action**: End with clear next steps for readers

### Citations
- Cite all factual claims, statistics, and quotes
- Prefer primary sources (government documents, official statements)
- Include URLs for online sources
- Format: [Source Name](URL) or "According to [Source]..."

## Content Requirements

### Must Include
- Local Goochland County angle or impact
- At least one call-to-action
- Proper source citations
- Professional featured image
- Clear headline and excerpt

### Must Avoid
- Personal attacks or ad hominem arguments
- Unsubstantiated claims or conspiracy theories
- Inflammatory or divisive language
- Content that could be construed as campaign coordination
- Plagiarism or uncited content

## Political Guidelines

### Do's
✓ Present conservative principles and policies clearly
✓ Cite Republican Party of Virginia positions
✓ Highlight local Republican achievements
✓ Explain how issues affect Goochland residents
✓ Provide factual criticism of policies (not individuals)
✓ Include opportunities for civic engagement

### Don'ts
✗ Make personal attacks on political opponents
✗ Use derogatory language or nicknames
✗ Spread misinformation or unverified claims
✗ Coordinate with specific candidate campaigns
✗ Use the newsletter for fundraising appeals

## Topics of Interest

### Priority Topics
1. Local Goochland County government and issues
2. Virginia state legislation and policy
3. Republican Party activities and events
4. Conservative policy analysis
5. Civic education and engagement
6. Community events and achievements

### Approach by Topic
- **Legislation**: Explain clearly, focus on local impact
- **Elections**: Educate on process, encourage participation
- **Policy**: Present conservative perspective with facts
- **Events**: Highlight opportunities for involvement
- **Community**: Celebrate local achievements and residents

## Example Standards

### Good Headline
"New Virginia Education Bill Gives Goochland Parents Greater School Choice"

### Weak Headline
"Democrats Continue Failed Education Policies"

### Good Opening
"Goochland County parents will soon have expanded options for their children's education thanks to a new state law passed last week. House Bill 1234 allows families to use education savings accounts to choose the best learning environment for their children."

### Weak Opening
"The liberals in Richmond finally did something right for a change, passing a school choice bill after conservatives fought for years."

## Quality Checklist

Before publishing, verify:
- [ ] Factually accurate with sources cited
- [ ] Relevant to Goochland residents
- [ ] Professional and respectful tone
- [ ] Clear local impact explained
- [ ] Call-to-action included
- [ ] No grammatical errors
- [ ] Appropriate length (600-1200 words)
- [ ] Professional image included
- [ ] Politically appropriate and non-inflammatory
